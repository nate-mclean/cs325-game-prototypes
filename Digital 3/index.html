Meteor Man

  • High Concept: Solve the mystery of your invisibility
  • Theme: Invisibility, Eavesdropping, Whatever it takes
  • Mandated Variety: Input: Mouse input. Randomness: Deterministic. Play style: Explorer. Genre: RPG.
  • Player Experience Goals: This is a puzzle RPG game. You walk around and interact with the houses and people inside. It has a funny storyline.
  • Gameplay: Click around to move. You can go into houses. Click to move through dialog. Try and solve the mystery.
  • Prototype Goal: A puzzle RPG like pokemon with a clever plot.
  • Features: Open world. Explore and do whatever you want. Storyline element that ties into the theme and has endgame. Dope music.
  • Setting/Premise: You are invisible!?? Why?? Explore your home town and solve the mystery!
  • Story: Without warning, you awoke one morning to find you were invisible. No one can help you, it's up to you to solve this mystery and figure out how to become visible again.
  • Target Audience: Players looking for a simple puzzle / RPG.
  • Play Time: Less than 5 minutes.
  • Strategies: Go into the houses and see what happens. Explore the entire world.
