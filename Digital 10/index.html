Silly sentence

  • High Concept: Build a silly sentence with a friend
  • Theme: Bookworm
  • Mandated Variety: Input: clickable buttons. Randomness: Random. Play style: Competitor, creator. Genre: puzzle.
  • Player Experience Goals: Multiplayer word game. Practice vocabulary and compete against a friend. Make silly sentences.
  • Gameplay: Click the letter tile you want to play. Play letter plays the letter and mvoes to next turn. You may start a new word IF it is a REAL word. Every play is checked against a dictionary.
  • Prototype Goal: A simple multiplayer word game
  • Features: Multiplayer. Word game. Fast gameplay.
  • Setting/Premise: Make a silly sentence, have fun!
  • Story: You and a friend are seeing who has a better vocabulary. This game will prove who it is.
  • Target Audience: Those that enjoy word or multiplayer games.
  • Play Time: Less than 5 minutes.
  • Strategies: Start with letters that are likely to make a word, like s, t, e, etc. End a word asap, don't let it get too complex.
