The pet shop

  • High Concept: Manage the pet store to glory
  • Theme: pet store
  • Mandated Variety: Input: clickable buttons. Randomness: Random + deterministic. Play style: Director. Genre: puzzle.
  • Player Experience Goals: This is a puzzle game. You must use your resources cleverly in order to help your pet store grow. If you upgrade to store 3/3 you win!
  • Gameplay: Click the buttons. Move through the turn.
  • Prototype Goal: A puzzle stategy-based game that involves financial managing.
  • Features: Fun cost / spend game, also simulate running a business.
  • Setting/Premise: Keep the pet store running
  • Story: You just started out with a new pet store Make the right decisions to expand!
  • Target Audience: Those that enjoy strategy puzzle games.
  • Play Time: Less than 5 minutes.
  • Strategies: Balance what you buy, or buy lots of fish. Upgrade rolls asap.
