Save the Trees

  • High Concept: Increase regulations to enable rapid tree growth
  • Theme: Preservationist
  • Mandated Variety: Input: clickable buttons. Randomness: Random + deterministic. Play style: Collector + Director. Genre: puzzle + overhead.
  • Player Experience Goals: This is a puzzle game. You must use your resources cleverly in order to increase regulations while keeping enough trees. Then once regulations are high, you can expand the forest rapidly.
  • Gameplay: Click the buttons. At the end of your turn, press advance year to attempt to fruit trees. Gain money by fruiting trees.
  • Prototype Goal: A puzzle stategy-based game that involves nature.
  • Features: Unique idea. Puzzle stategy game that is challenging. promoting enviromental conservation values.
  • Setting/Premise: The bulldozers are coming.. year after year.. Increase regulations to stop them!
  • Story: It is a constant struggle between mother nature and the humans ..until a liberal government with some legislative power steps in.. and with the increase of regulations, the spirit of mother nature will live on
  • Target Audience: Those that enjoy strategy puzzle games.
  • Play Time: Less than 5 minutes.
  • Strategies: Try and increase regulations as soon as possible. Try and save money, only plant as much as necessary.
  • Prototype Assets:
    • grass: Grass
    • Tree: tree
    • buttons: buttons
    • bulldozer : bulldozer
    • music: On the Nature of Daylight by Max Ritcher
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Made from scratch.
